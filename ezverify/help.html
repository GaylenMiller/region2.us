How do I get started?

EZ Verify has two steps to get started

  1. 1Browse to open spreadsheet 1
  2. 2Browse to open spreadsheet 2

What does the “Refresh” button do?

It closes and opens the spreadsheet

If a difference is found and corrected, the changes are not visible until the spreadsheet is closed and opened.

What is happening when I click one of the Event buttons?

EZ Verify compares all the columns, except the judge input columns, and displays any differences

The row for each student is compared until a difference is found. The compare stops so that the differences display is not filled with issues that will be fixed when the first problem is corrected.
